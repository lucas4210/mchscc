What we do

We compete. We teach. We ship.

Members learn by building in public. We enter hackathons, train for contests, and mentor younger students so the next wave can start sooner.

  • Hackathons

    Teams design and build full projects under real deadlines. This includes web apps, games, data tools, and hardware prototypes. You learn fast shipping, teamwork, and pitching.

    24 hr buildsproduct demosteamwork

  • Competitive Programming

    We practice algorithmic problem solving under time pressure. You train in data structures, complexity, and clean implementation. Prepares you for contest formats and tech interviews.

    algorithmsICPC stylespeed + accuracy

  • Middle School Outreach

    Members lead beginner coding workshops at local middle schools. We help students write first programs and simple games. Goal: show them that tech is for them.
